Brett Doyle

I'm a 20 year old 3D artist, currently living in London and looking for work as a Modeller in the Film/VFX industry, or environment/prop/modelling/texturing artist in the game industry

I've been doing 3D modelling since 2013. I first got interested in it while making custom maps & models for a game I used to play. Since then I've learnt and experimented with many different 3D tools & software (starting with 3DS Max), and also learnt a few programming languages which I've used for both 3D & 2D creative coding (as well as some web design & software tools). From the start I've wanted to use digital 3D as a artistic medium, and have studied to improve my artistic and technical ability. I most enjoy digital sculpture, and am inspired by artists such as Simon Stålenhag, Conrad Roset, and Pascal Blanché. My spare time is spent drawing, coding, and reading (mainly science fiction). I was home-schooled for most of my education, and in 2017 started studying with CG Spectrum, doing their Advanced 3D Modelling course. I finished in November 2018, during my time with CG Spectrum I learnt character & creature sculpting and greatly improved previously self-taught modelling & texturing skills.

Some of the work I did while at CG Spectrum can be seen on my ArtStation, and my programming projects are on GitHub.
