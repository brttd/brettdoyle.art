Sebulba

Sebulba from Star Wars, created with ZBrush, Maya, and Substance Painter. Made while studying with CGSpectrum, in the creature modelling & texturing part of their Advanced 3D Modelling Diploma. Rendered with Unreal Engine 4. High poly renders made with Arnold. Concept art from: characterdesignnotes.../interview-with-terryl-whitlatch-part_10 was used as reference.
