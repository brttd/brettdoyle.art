Rocket Launcher - Mk 153 SMAW

A model of the Mk 153 Shoulder-Launched Multipurpose Assault Weapon. Created with Maya and Substance Painter, rendered in Marmoset Toolbag. Made while studying with CGSpectrum, in the hard surface modelling part of their Advanced 3D Modelling Diploma.
