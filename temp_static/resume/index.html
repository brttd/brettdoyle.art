Summary

I've been learning 3D modelling for over 4 years. I've learnt and experimented with many different 3D tools & software, and learnt a few programming languages which I've used for 3D & 2D creative coding. Throughout I've studied to improve artistic and technical ability, inspired by artists such as Simon Stålenhag, Conrad Roset, and Pascal Blanché. I spend my spare time drawing, coding, or reading.
